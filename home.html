PROJECTS

The Truth Behind Us: I worked on this project with a group of girls from my GirlsWhoCode class. We found that discovering female role models has become difficult due to the lack of publicity they receive, so we decided to build a website which gives these amazing women a platform. The website is divided into different industries so users can have an experience more tailored to their interests. However, if the user is unsure about which one to choose we have a random button which generates a profile from any woman on the site. The use of bitmojis and pictures was intentional to simplify and stylize the site for younger users. I had an amazing time working on this project, to visit the page click the image to the right!
